Welcome to my portfolio! I’m Rev (Revanth Posina), a passionate Data Engineer with a Master of Science in Data Science from Indiana University Bloomington. My career has been driven by a love for transforming complex data into meaningful insights that fuel business growth and strategic decision-making.

Currently, I am working as a Database Engineer I at Bloom Insurance. Here, I focus on building scalable ETL pipelines, refining stored procedures, and optimizing data processes to ensure faster, more reliable access to health plan data. This role allows me to work on large-scale data migration, improving retrieval speed, and leveraging AWS for cloud-based automation—enhancing both performance and scalability for healthcare stakeholders.

Before this, I gained invaluable experience from the same place (Bloom) as a Data Engineer Intern, where I was involved in creating over 100 SSIS packages and integrating complex data for client-facing reports. My contributions streamlined data flows, enhanced report accuracy, and played a pivotal role in improving business decision-making.

Earlier in my career, I worked in Data Engineering Operations - Software Engineer at Ivy Comptech, where I led multiple data migration projects, transferring millions of user records and conducting in-depth data analysis to optimize user engagement and enhance product offerings for global gaming platforms like BWIN and BetMGM. This experience sharpened my ability to work with big data and collaborate with cross-functional teams to deliver data-driven solutions.

In addition to my professional roles, I have completed academic projects ranging from image fusion techniques, which were published by Springer, to sentiment analysis on Reddit NFT discourse. These experiences demonstrate my commitment to applying data science in various contexts to solve real-world challenges.

This portfolio is a reflection of my journey, and I’m excited to share the work that drives my passion for data. When I'm not working with data, I enjoy exploring topics like space, technology, comics, and anime—always eager to learn and watch something new ;)
